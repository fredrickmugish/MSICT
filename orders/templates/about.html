About MSICT ORS

Digitizing the equipment requisition process for the Military School of Information and Communication Technology

Our Digital Solution

The MSICT Offices Requirement Ordering System (ORS) transforms traditional paper-based requisitions into a streamlined digital workflow, enhancing efficiency and transparency across all departments.

Efficiency

Reduces processing time from days to hours with automated workflows and notifications.

Transparency

Real-time tracking of request status from submission to final decision.

Security

Military-grade encryption and role-based access control for all sensitive data.

Our Workflow Process

MSICT ORS follows a clear approval chain that respects military structure and embraces digital efficiency.

1

Department Request

HZO offices and NDO departments submit equipment needs through the digital portal with complete specifications.

2

QM Verification

Store keeper reviews request validity, checks inventory availability, and prepares recommendations.

3

CO Approval

Head of Organization evaluates the request against operational priorities and budget considerations.

4

Feedback Loop

Decision and comments flow back through QM to the requesting department with a complete audit trail.

Key Stakeholders

The system serves all levels of MSICT's organizational structure with role-specific interfaces.

Commanding Officer

Final Approval Authority

Reviews and approves/rejects all equipment requests with strategic oversight.

Quartermaster

Inventory Manager

Manages stock levels, verifies requests, and coordinates with suppliers.

Department Heads

Request Initiators

Submit equipment needs for their teams with technical specifications.

Our Mission

To modernize MSICT's resource allocation processes through secure digital transformation, maintaining military discipline while achieving unprecedented efficiency in equipment requisition and approval workflows.
