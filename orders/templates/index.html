MSICT Offices Requirement Ordering System

Streamlining the process of equipment requests and approvals between departments, store keepers, and organization leadership

Get Started Learn More

Request Submission

Departments can easily submit their equipment requirements through a simple online form.

QM Review

Store keepers (QM) receive requests, verify availability, and forward to CO for approval.

CO Approval

Head of Organization (CO) reviews and approves/rejects requests with feedback.

Workflow Process

Department Request

HZO offices and NDO departments submit their equipment needs through the system.

QM Verification

The Store Keeper (QM) reviews the request for validity and availability.

CO Decision

The Head of Organization (CO) approves or rejects the request with feedback.

Feedback Loop

Status updates flow back through QM to the requesting department.
